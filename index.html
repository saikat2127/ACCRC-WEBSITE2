ACCRC INTRA RoboArk 2022

AUGUST
2022

ACCRC Intra RoboArk 2022 was an inter-department robotics competition organized by the Adamjee Cantonment College Robotics Club. The event aimed to ignite innovation, teamwork, and technical skills among students by challenging them to design and operate their own robots. Featuring exciting segments like maze runners, line followers, and battle bots, RoboArk 2022 served as a dynamic platform for young tech enthusiasts to showcase their creativity and engineering talent.

ACCRC INTRA RoboArk 2023

JUNE
2023

ACCRC Intra RoboArk 2023 was a dynamic showcase of innovation and engineering hosted by the Adamjee Cantonment College Robotics Club. Focused on hands-on creativity and technical excellence, the event brought together students from various departments to compete in thrilling challenges like line following, maze solving, and AI-based robotics. RoboArk 2023 continued to foster a culture of problem-solving, collaboration, and futuristic thinking among the next generation of tech leaders.

HEATWAVE HACKATHON 2024

OCTOBER
2024

ACCRC Heatwave Hackathon 2024 was a high-intensity, innovation-driven coding and hardware challenge organized by the Adamjee Cantonment College Robotics Club. Designed to push creative boundaries, this 24-hour hackathon brought together aspiring developers, engineers, and tech enthusiasts to build impactful solutions under pressure. From AI-driven tools to smart robotics, Heatwave 2024 celebrated problem-solving, teamwork, and real-world tech application in a competitive yet collaborative environment.
